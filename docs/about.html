About

Rendoodle is the pioneering platform that digitizes national home inspection data to provide real-time repair and renovation cost estimates and online service orders. Through our exclusive home data partnerships and seamless integrations with key industry players, we offer a complete suite of home lifecycle services and products to homeowners via our market-leading platform.

Castle Partnership

peace of mind

Through an exclusive partnership with Castle Building Centres Group Ltd., we have a national footprint and a strong framework to support local businesses. The platform provides Consumers with everything they need to turn their home renovation doodles into reality, all while using Canada’s fastest growing independent buying group.

Castle Building Centres Group Ltd. has been proudly supporting Canadian homeowners for 57 years. As an industry leader of independent lumber and building materials dealers, Castle has over 300 independent member locations across Canada.

Consumers also have peace of mind knowing our Contractors are monitored to ensure they have current and proper insurance, licenses and training.
